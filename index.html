About Us

GeeksforGeeks (GFG) Chapter at VIT Bhopal is a community-driven initiative to develop the passion for technology, coding, and innovation in students. As an official chapter of GeeksforGeeks, one of the largest platforms for tech enthusiasts, our mission is to bridge the gap between classroom learning and industry requirements.
